Calculation:
Extension area = π × bore² ÷ 4
Retraction area = π × (bore² − rod²) ÷ 4
Force per cylinder = pressure × effective area × efficiency
Total force = force per cylinder × number of cylinders × mechanical ratio
Calculated band tension = total cylinder force ÷ 2. Pressure may be entered in bar or psi.
Assumption: The oven band wraps approximately 180° around the take-up drum, creating two tensioned band spans. Therefore, calculated band tension equals one-half of the total cylinder force. Verify any additional pulley, lever, linkage, or mechanical ratio used by the equipment. Confirm the published band strength with the band manufacturer and apply an appropriate safety margin.
