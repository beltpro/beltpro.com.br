Professional Lineage

Hands-On Training from Industry Leaders

BELTPRO's expertise has been built through years of hands-on training and field experience with some of the most respected and experienced professionals in the spiral conveyor industry. This knowledge has been complemented by more than a decade of practical experience supporting installations, commissioning, troubleshooting, inspections, maintenance, retrofits, and performance optimization for spiral conveyor belts and baking oven systems across the Americas.

Our technical approach is rooted in proven engineering principles, practical field experience, and a commitment to continuous learning. This professional lineage traces back to the pioneering innovations of Gerald Roinestad, whose work laid the foundation for modern spiral conveyor technology.
