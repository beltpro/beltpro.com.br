Calculation basis

Drum drive: industry-standard friction-drum spiral model using system radius, total linear load, number of tiers, support type and operating friction.

Secondary drive: simplified belt-pull calculation converted to shaft torque through the secondary sprocket pitch radius.

Power: calculated from torque and shaft speed, then corrected for transmission efficiency and service factor. The calculator selects the next standard motor size.

Preliminary sizing only. Final motor, gearbox, chain, shaft, bearing, acceleration, VFD and thermal selection must be checked against manufacturer ratings and the actual conveyor layout.
